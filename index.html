Key Takeaway

Firebase costs are essentially rounding error — the LearnedFoods collection adds ~$0.05/month across all 100 users. The real savings are entirely from avoided Claude calls: $79 → $15–22/month (~72–80% reduction).

The collection stabilizes fast because people are creatures of habit — most users rotate through ~50–80 foods. After a few hundred users, almost every common food is already learned.

Month 2+ is cheaper because most of the user's regular foods are already learned.
